From the ManufacturerHimalaya's Aloe & Cucumber Refreshing Body Lotion is a light and cool body lotion that hydrates and refreshes your skin without leaving it sticky. The fast-absorbing, daily-use body lotion is enriched with Aloe Vera and Cucumber, which leave your skin smooth and refreshed.Key ingredients:Aloe Vera, known for its many healing properties, is rich in polysaccharides and nutrients that exhibit antibacterial and antifungal action. A natural UV inhibitor, Aloe Vera's hydrating, softening and intense moisturizing properties nourish the skin.Cucumber cools, invigorates, refreshes and helps maintain skin's moisture. It keeps skin soft, has healing and soothing effects on damaged skin and is also a natural sunscreen. The abundant antioxidants in Cucumber rejuvenate the skin and leave it feeling soft and smooth.Directions for use:Apply Aloe & Cucumber Refreshing Body Lotion gently all over the body, slowly massaging it in.Best used after bathing when your skin is most receptive to hydration.

Welcome to Rupini`s

Rupini’s was established on the 2nd October 1994 as a sole proprietorship and later became a private limited company in 1997. The primary business of the company is to provide a wide range of bridal and beauty services. Rupini’s has successfully operated for 25 years.

Holistic beauty care being the central theme at Rupini’s, our philosophy entails the total grooming of a person, both physical and mental.

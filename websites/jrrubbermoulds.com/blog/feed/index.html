WHAT ARE PAVERS?

A paver is something that is commonly used as external flooring. They are usually made of concrete and is a brick-like piece. To make pavers, you mix concrete with some colouring agent and put it into a mould so it can be set and made into a certain shape.

Pavers are mostly used for walkways and outdoor platforms which is why it is commonly known as external flooring. There are different types of pavers that are used in construction.

Pavers are not something that your guests would normally tend to notice. The designs of your pavers are not paid much attention to.  But, pavers do play a very important role in the setting of your house.

They are usually used outside residential houses or commercial buildings. Most people use pavers for their parking lots.

Pavers usually help as it avoids the chipping of the surface and it makes it smooth and safe for people to walk about. This is why pavers are mostly used.

HERE ARE SOME REASONS FOR YOU TO INSTALL PAVERS

  • Value of your home.

Many time, real estate agents have suggested that when compared to homes that have ordinary concrete driveways, people prefer paved driveways as they look much neater.

It also adds value as it is much smoother for people to walk through. They usually tend to prefer interlocking paves over any other.

 

  • Durable.

Pavements are a very durable way of going about your driveway as they are resistant and hence cost effective. They can withstand any weather and also in case an earthquake or an erosion takes place.

Especially with interlocking pavers, they avoid movement taking place and they also accommodate weight which makes them much better than any other type of paver.

  • Drainage.

The pavers generally tend to have gaps between them. Especially while using brick pavers, they provide good drainage during heavy rains and floods.

They seep in the water between the gaps and do not let water stay on the surface for long. If water stays on the surface, then that would attract mosquitoes too. It could also make your driveway slippery.  

 

  • Benefits of a brick paver.

Pavers tend to beautify your driveway and adds that extra unnoticeable spark to your home/office.

Apart from the fact that it looks good, when you use brick pavers you can immediately run heavy machinery over it. So in a couple of days, you can drive over the pavers.

This is not possible when you use any paver other than brick paver.

  • Environmentally friendly.

The pavers are suggested to be an eco-friendly concept as when they drain in the water, beneficial bacteria remove unwanted toxins before they reach the beaches and the bay.

If you are using concrete or asphalt, rainwater tends to mix with various chemicals and with oil and goes into storm drains. In contrast to that, use the interlocking paving systems which will trap the oil and let the water seep out.  

 

  • Easy to replace.

Driveways are easier to replace when they are constructed with pavers as if there is any damage, then only the required pavers need to be removed and replaced.

Replacing concrete and asphalt is much more difficult and when it is done, it might look bad. With pavers, you wouldn’t even know when the damaged one is removed and replaced.

With pavers, multiple colours, sizes, shapes, and textures are available. It provides you with an unlimited artistic style as you can create it however you want.

But it is important to keep in mind that the thickness of the pavers should be according to its usage.

Different requirements have different thickness of pavers. Compared to walkways, a driveway would require a different thickness altogether.

USE OF PAVERS

Pavers are used in many scenarios. Pavers are mostly used as external flooring. They are mostly used for outdoor pavements in many parts of homes and commercial buildings. The most common uses of pavers are in driveways, walkways and even pool decks. Pavers are constructed in different colours and textures. DIFFERENT TYPES OF PAVERS

  • Brick Pavers.

Brick pavers have been used as surfaces for driveways mostly for years now. Brick pavers can be manufactured in several shapes and can be made into a range of colours. Brick pavers are usually made of natural materials and hence can be cleaned and reused which also makes it eco-friendly. It can be manufactured in colours and textures that you want and placed accordingly. Some disadvantages of brick pavers are that they aren’t very cost efficient. They require regular maintenance and can tend to chip off. The reason to which brick pavers are not very beneficial is that they are very fragile.

  • Concrete Pavers.

Concrete pavers are usually used as a flooring technique for driveways and walkways. The advantages of concrete pavers are that it gives a very natural look and it can be customized with the colours and shapes that you want. Another benefit of pavers is that it is cost-efficient and can be installed easily. Raw materials that are of low cost are used mostly as it is cheaper than using bricks. But also keep in mind that when artificial pigments are used, it can fade away quickly. Since raw materials are used, they can also not last long.

  • Rubber Pavers.

The biggest advantage of rubber pavers is that it is eco-friendly. It is recyclable and hence it is also considered to be cost friendly and efficient. But the disadvantage is that if the pavers get wet, then too much heat is absorbed which is not good for the pavers. Wet pavers tend to expand. But it is not very prone to cracking.

  • Plastic Pavers.

As the name suggests, these pavers are made of plastic. The plastic that is used to make these pavers are either new or reused plastic. But it is most beneficial if companies make use of recyclable plastic as that would remove the plastic waste in the economy. But the process of recycling is not very easy and hence it might be very expensive. This type of paper is used by many but it is not considered to be as durable as stone pavers.

  • Stone Pavers.

Stone pavers are slightly more different when compared to the other types of pavers. Stone pavers are different as they are not uniform like the other pavers. They are directly cut from quarries and used, unlike the others. The other pavers are moulded and manufactured. Hence the pavers tend to be identical. For stone pavers, they are not uniform and there is a variation in the size, colour, and texture.

  • Marble.

These pavers provide a sense of elegance and beauty to the flooring. They are manufactured when limestones are exposed to a certain temperature. It gives a sophisticated and natural look to the driveways or walkways of your home or commercial area. They may cause discolouration quickly and as marble is a great conductor of heat, the floor also tends to absorb more heat especially during the summer time.

WHO IS A PAVING CONTRACTOR?

From working on constructing paved surfaces for roads and houses and other small to large projects, paving contractors are hired to construct such surfaces and to work on existing ones that require repair.

Most paving companies make it a point to mention that they specialize in either residential or commercial paving. They use large equipment to complete the paving process – some might use excavators to lay out the land before the paving is done and some might use paving machines to place the tiles.

Sometimes they also use tamping machines which are mostly for flattening the surface.

Why do you require a paving constructor?

Small paving jobs can usually be done by yourself but otherwise, for large scale projects, you will need the help of a paving contractor.

This is not only because of the fact that large scale projects are for experienced contractors, but it’s also because they have access to better types of machinery that would help the process.

5 QUESTIONS THAT YOU NEED TO ASK YOUR PAVING CONSTRUCTOR

1. Ask them for how long they have been a paving contractor and some examples of their previous work.

If a company has been functioning for quite some time now, it does not always have to mean that their work is perfect. But it is a point that if they have been in business for a while now, then their work might be good enough for them to last so long.

It is also important that other factors are looked into before you trust the contractor. You can also ask him for some previous work that they have done.

Ask their former clients how their experience was after hiring these contractors and also try and see the work that they’ve done before.

2. Make sure that they are insured.

Ask and confirm if the contractors you are hiring are insured. If not, then any sort of damages that are caused to your property by the contractor would be at your expense.

When you hire your contractors, ask for proof of insurance.

Make sure that they have public liability insurance and workers compensation insurance. Public liability insurance covers any third party liabilities out of the premises and any legal costs that arise out of it.

3. Ask the contractor for the total estimate.

It is important that you ask your contractor for some details about your final payment. Your contractor should be able to give you proper details of the final amount that you ought to pay him and not just an estimate.

He should also inform you of any additional payments as no payments should come as a surprise to you later.

4. Details in the scope of work document

This is a document that is basically an agreement on the work that is going to be performed in the project. It’s basically an understanding of the scope of the entire project.

When you are collecting bids from multiple organizations, it is possible that you get confused because certain companies do not provide a proper detailed scope of work document.

So make sure that you chose the right company with a detailed scope of work document.

5. Try to find references you can call and contact

You needn’t believe everything a contractor tells you about his work. He is only going to let you know about the positives and not the negatives.

So try and get some good references that you can contact. These references could be some previous clients. If clients have good feedback about the company, then there’s nothing to worry about.

WET CASTE PAVEMENT
